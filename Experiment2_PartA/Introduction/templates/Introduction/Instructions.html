Experiment

You are going to participate in a financial exchange game. The currency used is ECU (Experimental Currency Unit).
The exchange rate is as follows.

1 ECU = ${{session.real_world_currency_per_point}}

All participants are divided into two groups, A and B. Each participant is randomly assigned a partner from the other group.
At the beginning Player A is granted a specified initial amount. From this amount Player A can send a part to his partner from group B. It is possible to choose any amount between zero and the total amount. This amount is tripled by the experimenter and passed on to Player B. Player B now decides which part of the tripled amount he sends back to Player A.

The transaction is going to take place exclusively between you and your partner from the other participant group. The only influence from outside comes from the experimenter in tripling the amount.

Payoff

Your payoff only depends on your decision and the decision of your partner.

Player A: Initial amount – amount sent to B + amount received back from B
Player B: Initial amount + triple the amount sent from A – amount sent back to A
